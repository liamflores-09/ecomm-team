Getting Started
How to log in, navigate, and set up your account.
Logging In
1

Open the App

Navigate to the app URL in your browser. You'll land on the login screen if you're not already signed in.

2

Enter Your Credentials

Type your username and password provided by your manager. Usernames are typically your first name in lowercase.

3

You're In

You'll land on your Dashboard. The sidebar and available features depend entirely on your assigned role.

Forgot your password? Contact your Manager or Ecomm Head directly via Viber. There is no self-service password reset.
Logging Out
Click your avatar in the top-right corner of any page. A dropdown will show a Logout option. Always log out when using a shared device.
Roles & Permissions
Every account has a role that controls exactly what you can see and do.
HeadAdmin

Ecomm department leader with complete access — all admin tools, all features, all team data. Can manage users, view all reports, and override decisions.

ManagerAdmin

Team manager with full admin access identical to Head. Manages day-to-day operations, onboards users, and monitors team health.

Analyst

Brand catalog specialist. Can create, edit, and delete catalogs and post announcements. No EOD reports or calendar access. Dashboard shows catalog KPIs.

Researcher

Product researcher who can manage brand catalogs. Submits daily EOD reports, uses calendar and tools. Focus is product research and catalog accuracy.

Content

Content/posting team with access to all posting tools — Posting Procedure, Data Gathering, Price Calculator. Submits detailed EOD reports tracking SKU counts.

Graphics

Design team handling CVP banners, product images, and visual assets. Submits EOD reports, uses calendar and tools.

Backend

Backend team handling bulk uploads, cross-listing, and quality control. Submits EOD reports, uses calendar and tools.

Creating an Announcement
HeadManagerAnalyst
1

Click "New Announcement"

The button appears in the top-right of the Announcements page.

2

Fill in the form

  • Title — Short, clear headline
  • Message — Full announcement body text
  • Expires At — Auto-set to 7 days from now. Clear it for a permanent notice.
  • Pin — Toggle to pin this notice to the top for all team members
3

Submit

The announcement is immediately visible to all team members.

Pinned announcements stay at the top of the list for everyone. Use sparingly — only for truly time-sensitive notices.

Adding a Catalog
HeadManagerAnalystResearcher
1

Button in the top-right of the Brand Catalogs page.

2

Fill in the modal

  • Brand — Must be created first by admin in Brands Management
  • Title — Name of this catalog entry (e.g. "Q2 2026 Product List")
  • Status — Available, Upcoming, or Seasonal
  • Notes — Additional context or instructions

You must provide either an external link or a file upload. At least one is required before the form can be saved.

Task FieldsContent team labels · other roles may differ
T1

New SKU

Count of new parent or single products posted today. Each unique product = 1. Do not count variations here.

T2

Variation SKU

Count of child/variant products posted today. Each colour, size, or variation = 1.

T3

Data Gathering

Total SKUs researched — collecting specs, images, and details needed before posting.

T4

Update Listings

Existing product listings updated today — photos, descriptions, long descriptions, corrections, etc.

T5

Other Tasks

Miscellaneous work — Canva design, pending SKU processing, cross-listing, QC. Describe briefly in Remarks.

Notifications
System alerts shown via the bell icon in the top bar.

The bell icon shows a badge count of unread notifications. The panel auto-refreshes every 30 seconds. Opening it marks all as read.

Notification Types

EOD Submitted

Admins only. Triggered when any member submits their end-of-day report.

Calendar Event

Sent to attendees (or everyone if no specific attendees set) when a new calendar event is created.

Calendar Task Assigned

Sent to members of the assigned role when a new calendar task is created for them.

Calendar Task Completed

Sent to managers/heads and the task creator when a parent task is marked complete.

New Brand Catalog

Sent to all users when a new brand catalog is added to the system.

Mark All Read
Marks every notification as read at once
Clear All
Permanently removes all notifications
Delete One
Remove a single notification from the list
Posting Procedure
Step-by-step reference guide for posting products correctly.

Accordion Layout

Steps are displayed as collapsible cards — click any step to expand and read the full instructions with examples.

Resource Links

Each step includes embedded resource links and guides relevant to that stage of the posting process.

Who Uses It

Primarily the Content team, but visible and accessible to all roles as a reference document.

Requirements
Platform-specific listing rules. Content team.
A reference page of platform-by-platform rules to follow when listing a product — title formatting, required fields, image specs, and other constraints that differ between Lazada, Shopify, Shopee, and TikTok. Check this before posting anything you're unsure about.

SLA & Weekly Output
Month-over-month SLA comparison, broken down week by week.
